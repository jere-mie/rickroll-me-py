How It All Works

Everything You Need to Know to Get Started!



What is This?

This is an app that let's you set up custom links that redirect to your website of choice.

Think of bit.ly, except you can customize your own link to be whatever you want!


What Makes This App Special?

This application not only allows you to customize your redirect link, but also the meta data of the link.

This makes it especially useful for pranks, namely the Rick Roll meme, where you can send someone a link that says, perhaps, that an interesting news story has come out, when in reality they click the link and it redirects to an 80's pop song.




Ok, I'm In, How Do I Get Started?

First, you'll need to go to the new link page, where you can customize a new link.

You'll be greeted by a form with the following fields:

  • URL to Redirect To

    This is the place where you put the destination of your redirect, such as an 80's music video

  • Custom URL Ending

    This will be the part after the domain, like so: allnewsnow.online/l/[your_link_here]

  • Site Name

    The name of the supposed website you want to use, for example, "News Today"

  • Site Title

    This would be the title of an article or such, for example, "New iPhone Ditches Charging Port"

  • Description

    A description of what's on the website, usually one or two sentences

  • Link to Image

    This is the url to the meta image you want displayed when someone pastes your link in a chatroom or so. You can often copy the url of images from places like Google Images
